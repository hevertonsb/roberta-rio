    Roberta do Rio

    Roberta believes jewellery should bring colour and poetry to your world. Her designs combine rich natural gemstones, many of which are native to her homeland, with high polish gold. Playing with translucent cut gems and hand carved opaque stones, these colourful jewels are the main protagonists in her creative process

    The touch of the human hand is present throughout the design process, jewels begin life as ideas in sketchbooks. This personal process imbues a little of the designer into every creation, enabling Roberta to ensure each piece is made just as she envisioned. More
